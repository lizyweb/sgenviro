About us

Pollution Control Equipment

Established in 2008, SG Enviro Technologies has been dedicated to manufacturing, supplying, and exporting a comprehensive range of Air Pollution Control Equipments, Waste Water Treatment Plants, ETP, STP, RO Plant, and their spares. As a prominent player in the industry, we are recognized for our commitment to producing high-quality Air Pollution Control Equipment that adheres to the stringent pollution control norms set by CPCB. Our equipment stands out for its robust construction, superior quality, and extended lifespan, earning accolades from our clients.

We take pride in being counted among the leading players in the field, serving as a Manufacturer and Supplier of Air Pollution Control Equipment. Committed to excellence, our products are crafted with the finest materials, ensuring compliance with industrial CPCB pollution control standards. Renowned for their durability, top-notch quality, and longevity, our Air Pollution Control Equipment enjoys widespread appreciation among clients, further solidifying our position in the industry.
